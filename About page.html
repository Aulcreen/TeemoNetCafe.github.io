About us



As gamers ourselves, we've decided to come together and make a cafe where people just like us could come and play games. To have fun. To de-stress from life.

We've got a wide variety of games patched and updated, fast internet connections, and our equipment's pretty up-to-date. If you're interested, we also sell parts in which you can have in your personal computer at home.



In this cafe, you're not just limited to just playing. Here you can come do your work.

You can also got to sleep or even read if you just want to be in a quiet place.

If you're also hungry, you can bring your own food and drinks as long as you keep the place clean. You can also contact our staff and order food from our menu. Don't be afraid to ask for help if the computer's not working right. They're pretty friendly.



We strive to cater to fellow gamers like us.
